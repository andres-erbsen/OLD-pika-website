CPW Events

Event Time Description



Thursday 4/7

Tie Dye T Shirts 5:00 PM - 6:30 PM Tie dye your shirt before Thai dining! Bring your own shirt (or you can choose from one of ours).
Thai Dining 6:30 PM - 7:30 PM Thai Iced Tea? Thai Food? Yum! Join us for a delicious Thai feast (with vegan options included!).
Not your parents' sex talk 10:00 PM - 11:00 PM Let's talk about sex. "Hookup culture", safer sex, BDSM, polyamory, sex positivity, birth control, the November rule, consent, and STIs.



Friday 4/8

Shoeblade and Forkapple 5:00 PM - 6:00 PM Ye of youthful vigor, test thy mettle by playing these noble sports of pikans of yore. No skill necessary. Also ... learn about pika lore, the Egg of Consensus, the Day of Black Fire, the MURPHUS BOOKUS, and other topics of interest in pika's rich history!
Roll your own sushi! 6:00 PM - 8:00 PM Roll your own (sushi)! We will provide all of the components: seasoned rice, seaweed, beautifully prepared vegetables, and, perhaps, raw dead fish...
Jam! Session 8:30 PM - 9:30 PM Bring your own instrument (BYOI) encouraged! Or, you can use some of ours! Sit back or lean forward and make some beautiful sounds together. No experience necessary.
Poetry and Puns Circle 9:30 PM - 10:30 PM Bring your favorite poetry books or your own poetry, or read some of ours! Hopefully this won't be a cat-astrophe... ;)
Smoothies on Deck 5:00 PM - 11:00 PM Ahoy there lad! Git ov'r here to blend all the fruits of the World into tasty smoothies, milkshakes, slushies, frappes, whatever ye heart desires!
Tea Blending at pika 5:00 PM - 11:00 PM Blend teas with pika's wonderful collection of herbs! Pick some aromatic leaves from the garden, if you want. Then, drink them right there on the spot. Or, choose from our selection of bagged teas and relax from a day of exploration!



Saturday 4/9

Meditation with chickens 10:00 PM - 11:00 PM Learn some simple breathing techniques, explore the mind-breath connection and meditate! Learn how the breath can affect the state of our mind, reduce stress and enjoy some guided meditation!
Happy Chickens Breakfast! 11:00 PM - 12:00 PM Come meet our chickens and have a fantastic vegan breakfast!
Run 4 Fun 12:00 PM - 1:00 PM Want to explore Cambridgeport/the river on a fun jog-about the Pika area? A great opporutnity to get to know the area around MIT!
Art and Mural Drawing 1:00 PM - 4:00 PM Make art for pika walls, or make entire murals ! Draw on the wall, make a revered painting/drawing for our pika art gallery
Learn to Spin 2:00 PM - 3:00 PM Patience you must have, my young Padawan, and you too will learn how to spin a glow staff!
Yoga with cats 3:00 PM - 4:00 PM ommmmmmmmm. Vinyasa flow your college decision stresses away, here at pika! And hang out with Desi and Lilah, our cats!
Heresy, Hair Dyeing, and Henna 4:30 PM - 5:30 PM Modify one or more hairs on your head with colors and/or scissors. We will help you realize your hair dream :)
Drag Rainbow Dinner 8:00 PM - 9:00 PM Everyone is welcome at this dinner, dressed up or not! You can get dressed up in our outrageous clothing! Or, get your makeup done! You will look fabulous. Come by early if you want to help cook!
LGBTQ Night 9:00 PM - 10:00 PM We'll have a relaxed talk about all sorts of LGBTQ issues, life at MIT, dating, sexuality, gender, coming out, the whole nine yards.
