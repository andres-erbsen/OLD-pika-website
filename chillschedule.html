Saturday 9/3

Art and Mural Drawing 5:00 PM - 6:00 PM Make art for pika wall, or make entire murals! Draw on the wall, make a revered painting/drawing for our pika art gallery
Make your own super lightweight camping stove! 6:00 PM - 7:00 PM Dining plans (except for pika's) are for fools. Sharing a kitchen is for tools. Let's be Real Hardk0re (TM).

pika House Tours 8:00 PM - 9:00PM Try our fire pole, see our multi-story deck, and learn about pika lore: the Egg of Consensus, the Day of Black Fire, the MURPHUS BOOKUS, and other topics of interest in pika's rich history! If you miss this event we'll be giving more tours throughout the week.
Tea Blending and Consumption Party!! 9:00 PM - 10:00 PM Blend teas with pika's wonderful collection of herbs! Pick some aromatic leaves from the garden, if you want. Then, drink them right there on the spot.

Heresy and Hair Dyeing 5:00 PM - 6:00 PM Modify one or more hairs on your head. We will help you realize your hair dream :)
Facepaint, Henna, and Temporary Tattoos 6:00 PM - 7:00 PM Let's be body positive and get artsy!
Ristorante Italiano 7:00 PM - 8:00 PM Dinner! Simply speaking, an extravaganza of Italian food. Come any time before 7pm if you want to help cook! Meat and vegan options available.
Forkapple and Shoeblade 8:00 PM - 9:00 PM Ye of youthful vigor, test thy mettle by playing these noble sports of pikans of yore. No skill necessary.

Roll your own Sushi 5:00 PM - 7:00 PM Roll your own (sushi)! We will provide all of the components: seasoned rice, seaweed, beautifully prepared vegetables, and, perhaps, raw dead fish...
Sushiya 7:00 PM - 8:00 PM Eat your own (sushi!)
Jam Sesh 8:00 PM - 9:00 PM Bring your own instrument (BYOI) encouraged! Or, you can use some of ours! Sit back or lean forward and make some beautiful sounds together. No experience necessary.
Anime Night 9:00 PM - 10:00 PM Are you the type of person who rages at bad English translations? Or do you never watch anime, silently judging those who do (but secretly wonder what it's all about)? Either way, come check out our TV room, watch some anime with us, and have a good time!

DIY Fashion 5:00 PM - 6:00 PM Cut your t-shirt, bedazzle your bra, screenprint, and more!
Drag Dress-Up 6:00 PM - 7:00 PM Guess who has a drag closet? pika!!! Get dressed up in our Outrageous Clothing! Get your makeup done! You will look fabulous.

Poetry Open Mic 9:00 PM - 10:00 PM "Bring your favorite poetry books or your own poetry, or read some of ours!

If you do not like talking or listening to music this event might not be for you. If you do not like me this event might not be for you. Suggested dress code: dark like my future.
